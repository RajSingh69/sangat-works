About Sangat Works

Built to help the Sikh community support itself.

Sangat Works is a community platform for finding, supporting and recommending Sikh businesses, trades and professionals.

Why this exists

Many of us know Sikh electricians, plumbers, accountants, tutors, developers, mortgage advisors and other professionals, but there is no simple central place to find them.

Sangat Works aims to make that easier by giving the community one trusted place to discover services, share reviews, build connections and keep opportunities within the Sangat where possible.

Support Sikh businesses

Help local Sikh-owned businesses and self-employed professionals become easier to find.

Build trust

Profiles, reviews, verification badges and community links help people choose with confidence.

Strengthen the Sangat

The long-term goal is stronger community support, more referrals and better local connections.

Sangat Works — built for community support.
