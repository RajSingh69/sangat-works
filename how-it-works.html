How It Works

Simple to join. Simple to use.

Create a profile, search the directory, read reviews and support trusted members of the Sangat.

For members

1. Create your account

Join using email login, then create your personal or business profile.

2. Build your profile

Add your services, tags, experience, photos, logo, links, Gurdwara association and contact preferences.

3. Become searchable

Once your profile is public, others can find you by trade, skill, town, service area or keyword.

4. Receive reviews

Other logged-in members can leave reviews and recommendations on your profile.

5. Request verification

Verification badges help show trust, business legitimacy and community connection.

6. Support the Sangat

Use the platform to find services, recommend good people and keep opportunities within the community.

For admins

Admins can review users, manage verification badges, feature trusted profiles and monitor platform activity.
