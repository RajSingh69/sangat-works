About the Developers

The people behind Sangat Works.

Sangat Works was created by Rajan and Jags with a shared aim: to bring the Sangat together and support one another in a practical, trusted and financially meaningful way.

Why we built Sangat Works

We saw a gap in the market for a platform that helps Sikhs find, recommend and support one another’s businesses, trades, services and skills.

Sangat Works is designed to help keep opportunities within the community, build trusted connections and create a stronger support network across the Sangat.
